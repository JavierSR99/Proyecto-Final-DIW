ALOVERA SUMMER BEACH FESTIVAL

THE BIGGEST SUMMER FEST IN SPAIN
'Alovera Summer Beach Festival' started as a tradition that locals used to enjoy during the last week of summer. As the years went, people from near villages decided to go and join the party, it was so so good that since then, everybody wants to go there and party hard. 20 year after, it has become the biggest summer festival in its country and one of the best and most awarded in the planet, having hosted more than 40,000 people in just 4 days. Amazing, isn't it?
